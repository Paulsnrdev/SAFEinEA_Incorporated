About SAFEinEA Incorporated

SAFEinEA Incorporated is a leading Environmental Health and Safety Consulting and Training Firm dedicated to helping Canadian businesses create safer, healthier workplaces. Founded by Bamidele Oni, a seasoned safety professional with extensive qualifications and experience, we bring expertise and passion to every project.

Our Mission

To empower organizations with the knowledge, tools, and strategies needed to protect their most valuable asset their people while ensuring regulatory compliance and operational excellence.

Our Approach

We believe in practical, scalable solutions that integrate seamlessly with your operations. Our services are customized to your industry, size, and specific challenges, ensuring maximum effectiveness and return on investment.

Our Founder

Bamidele Oni, MSc, Dip.OHS, Dip.THSc, EPt

As the founder and CEO of SAFEinEA Incorporated, Bamidele brings a wealth of knowledge and hands-on experience in occupational health and safety across multiple industries.

With advanced degrees and certifications, including a Master of Science degree and multiple diplomas in occupational health and safety, Bamidele is committed to advancing workplace safety standards across Canada.
