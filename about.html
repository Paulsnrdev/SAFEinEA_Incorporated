About SAFEinEA Incorporated

SAFEinEA Incorporated is a leading Environmental Health and Safety Consulting and Training Firm dedicated to helping Canadian businesses create safer, healthier workplaces. Founded by Bamidele Oni, a seasoned safety and health professional with extensive combination of qualifications and experience, in the field of Environmental Health, Public Health, Occupational Health and Safety and Environmental Management.

Added to this, he is an educationist, and proficient in curriculum development. As a lecturer, he has facilitated several trainings and also coached student from basis to professionalism in colleges and professional institutions. We bring expertise and passion to every project.

His experience as an enforcement officer, who worked as provincial safety and health officer, positioned him to understand what your workplace needs to comply with legislative requirements in various industries.

SAFEinEA Incorporated provides Environmental Health and Safety consulting and training services. Our offerings include SAFE Work Manitoba endorsed trainings, customized on-site programs, advisory services, and the development of safety programs. We also partner with training institutions to deliver basic academic lecture as electives or main course to equip student with safety and health orientation regardless of their field of learning.

SAFEinEA is accredited training provider in Manitoba and assists businesses in meeting legislative requirements and maintaining safe work environments.

Our Mission

To empower organizations with the knowledge, tools, and strategies needed to protect their most valuable asset - their people, while ensuring regulatory compliance and smooth operation without disruptions.

Our Approach

We believe in practical, scalable solutions that integrate seamlessly with your operations. Our services and training uphold general principles of occupational health and safety but customizable to your workplace size, and specific challenges, ensuring a work environment that is both safe and healthy.

Our Founder

Bamidele Oni, MSc, Dip.OHS, Dip.THSc, EPt

As the founder and CEO of SAFEinEA Incorporated, Bamidele brings a wealth of knowledge and hands-on experience in occupational health and safety across multiple industries.

With advanced degrees and certifications, including a Master of Science degree and multiple diplomas in occupational health and safety, Bamidele is committed to advancing workplace safety standards across Canada.
