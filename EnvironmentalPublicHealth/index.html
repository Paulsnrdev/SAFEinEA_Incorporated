Why work with us

We combine accredited laboratory testing, field sampling expertise and community health practice to deliver environmental health and risk assessment services across Canada.

Our teams design monitoring programs and preventive controls that align with federal and provincial frameworks including the Guidelines for Canadian Drinking Water Quality, Canadian Environmental Quality Guidelines, Canadian Ambient Air Quality Standards, the Safe Food for Canadians Regulations, and provincial public health standards, so clients meet both scientific best practice and regulatory expectations.

Water Quality Testing

Sampling, microbiological and chemical analysis, source-to-tap risk assessment.

Deliverables:

Lab reports, compliance summaries, corrective action plans.

Air Quality Sampling

"Real-time and integrated air monitoring for healthier indoor and outdoor environments."

  • Ambient and indoor sampling
  • PM2.5/PM10, VOCs, NOx, SO2, CO monitoring

Deliverables:

Time-series data, exceedance reports, mitigation roadmaps.

Community Mobilization and Wellness

"Engaging communities to turn data into action."

Stakeholder engagement, workshops, Indigenous and municipal consultation, behaviour-change campaigns.

Deliverables:

Workshop materials, community action plans, evaluation reports, e-learning.

Housekeeping and Facility Hygiene

"Operational hygiene that protects staff and visitors."

Cleaning audits, SOP development, PPE guidance, infection prevention protocols for facilities.

Deliverables:

Audit checklists, training modules, cleaning schedules.

Food Safety Training

"Practical training that keeps kitchens compliant and customers safe."

HACCP/PCP support, food handler courses, allergen management.

Deliverables:

Certificates, training records, preventive control plans.
