Our Services

We offer a comprehensive range of environmental health and safety services tailored to meet your organization's specific needs.

Customized and Tailored Safety Training

Participate in specialized safety training courses that are developed to meet your specific organizational needs, ensuring employees are equipped with necessary safety skills and knowledge.

Safety and Health Program Development

Develop and implement customized safety and health programs tailored to your organization's needs, ensuring compliance with relevant legislations and promoting a safe workplace.

On-Site Safety Consulting and Training

Receive professional training and consulting on-site to assess and enhance your organization's environmental health and safety standards, aimed at achieving compliance and improved workplace safety.

SAFE Work Manitoba Endorsed Training

We deliver on the SAFE Work Manitoba Endorsed Trainings, Workplace Safety and Health Committee/Representative, Supervisor Safety and Health Roles and Responsibilities, Hazard Identification and Risk Control, Workplace Incident Investigation.

Inspection and Investigation

Onsite inspection and investigation services that is driven by comprehensive understanding of legislative provision and previous field enforcement experience.

New Workers Orientation

The enthusiasm that new workers bring to work sometimes put them at risk of threading terrains they are not familiar with. Despite having technical skill and required professional expertise, which influenced why your workplace hired them. It is important to bring them to speed on safe ways of doing what they know how to do. So, we can adapt our new workers safety orientation to your specific workplace need.
